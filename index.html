GIAIC

GOVERNOR INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING


The Governor Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (GIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by the Governor of Sindh, Kamran Khan Tissory, Sir.Zia Khan, Sir.Danayal Nagori and Sir.Ameen Alam. The goal is to promote education, research, and business opportunities in emerging tech fields such as:

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet Of Things


Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares students to program in open-source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native. It introduces them to the development of Microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence:

The Artificial Intelligence program takes students through the foundation of AI technology. It involves programming in open-source languages such as Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0, and Flask. The program covers deploying practical AI solutions and use cases, and provides real-life examples of modern-day AI in practice.

Blockchain:

The Blockchain program emphasizes understanding blockchains such as Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric). Students learn to program in languages such as Solidity and JavaScript on frameworks like Node.js. They are introduced to the development of Distributed Apps (DApps) using frameworks like Web3 and Truffle, along with Blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS) and Initial Coin Offering (ICO) of crypto-tokens.

Internet of Things:

The Internet of Things (IoT) program introduces students to open-source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes. The program makes them proficient in developing Microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
